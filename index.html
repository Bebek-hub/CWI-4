Segun Ariyo

Segun Ariyo is a Nigerian Christian leader, a disciple-maker,author, Pastor, and renowned teacher of the Word of God. He holds discipleship retreats, Bible studies, and a once in a year inter-deni=ominational fellowship that attracts Christians of all backgrounds at the close of the year. He is an itinerant preacher who responds to ministrations based on invitations and spiritual permits.

Education

Segun Ariyo grew and lived with his parents in Ijebu Ode from birth up till their relocation to Ibadan in 1966. He graduated from the polytechnic of Ibadan in Nigeria where he studied Accountancy between 1982 and 1985,

Segun Ariyo also completed his studies in Bible College from the Life International College of Theology, Ibadan, Nigeria.

Biography

Segun Ariyo was born on the 29th of April 1963 to Reverend Ariyo and Mrs. Ariyo. He was the only child of the parent and was born to them whilst they were already in their advanced years.

Segun Ariyo was called into ministry in 1986, he coordinates Potter's House Christian Mission, a non-denominational Christian Ministry.
